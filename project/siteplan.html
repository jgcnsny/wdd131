SEVENTEEN Online Portfolio Site Plan

Site Name: SEVENTEEN Online Portfolio

Purpose

SEVENTEEN Online Portfolio website is to showcase a curated collection of creative works, professional achievements, and personal projects, highlighting the skills, talents, and expertise of the artist.

The SEVENTEEN Online Portfolio serves as a dynamic and visually engaging platform for artist branding, professional networking, and inspiring collaboration opportunities.

Scenarios

  • What makes SEVENTEEN unique as a boy band, and where can I learn more about their music, members, and journey?
  • How can I stay updated with SEVENTEEN's latest news, upcoming events, and interact with the fandom?
